Our Story

Born from a Passion
for Refined Living

Lumière Interior Studio was founded on a simple belief: every home deserves to feel extraordinary. We work at the intersection of architecture, artistry, and lifestyle — translating your vision into spaces that are both beautiful and deeply personal.

With over seven years and 240+ completed residences across Mumbai, Pune, and beyond, our studio has earned a reputation for meticulous craftsmanship, thoughtful material selection, and an unfailing eye for proportion and light.

We never start with a template. We start with you.

Priya Mehta Founder & Principal Designer
